Data Deletion Request

Pet+ stores all your data locally on your device. We do not operate servers that hold your personal data. Here is how to delete your data completely.

Option 1 — Delete data on your device

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps → Pet+
  3. Tap Storage → Clear Data

This permanently removes all pets, reminders, notes, and photos stored by the app.

Option 2 — Uninstall the app

Uninstalling Pet+ removes all locally stored data from your device.

Option 3 — Delete your Google Drive backup

If you enabled the Google Drive backup feature:

  1. Open Google Drive
  2. Find the folder named Pet+
  3. Delete the folder and empty the Trash

If you need additional help or want to submit a deletion request by email, contact us:

support@tombiksstudio.com

We will respond to email deletion requests within 30 days.
